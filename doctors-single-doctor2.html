Biography

Over the last 20 years, Dr. Chandan Kumar H N at Kuvempunagar has offered countless patients excellent treatment for various health issues. Having trained and served for 12 years at prestigious Apollo BGS Hospital and since last 8 years at Bhanavi Hospital, He has become a neighborhood name at Mysore.

He has not only catered to thousands of outpatients but also to a wide variety of in patients including critically ill cases in ICU. involvement and dedicated services during covid pandemic waves have not only drawn in patients from in and around the city but from across the state as well.

He had the opportunity to train and work in Apollo Hospitals group from the very next day of his MBBS degree completion and worked in all subspecialities of medicine with special focus on Diabetes, critical care and Gastroenterology.
He was a dedicated, hardworking post graduate student, favorite of all his medical teachers and won many inter hospital Medical Quiz and competitions and Presented many medical papers in multiple CMEs both local and at state level conferences.

With his dedication to work and passion to learn, He became the first DNB student to pass out in first attempt from Apollo BGS Hospitals Mysore. In addition to fine tuning his skills in all medical bedside procedures he also was well trained in handling artificial ventilation, Diagnostic GI Endoscopy, bedside Ultrasonography and Screening Echocardiography which are added beneficiary tools to a dynamic Physician to manage challenging complex medical cases.
