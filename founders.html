Welcome to Leela Clinic!

First of all, thanks for taking out time to visit our website and know about us.

I always thought of having a clean, spacious, calm clinic space with pleasing ambience and healing vibes to practice medicine’ a sacred profession that I love. Mysuru, the city of palaces deserves such updated health care infra structures with world class ambience and services for its people. Hence the “Leela Clinic”.

Named after my mother, it is one of its kind multispecialty clinic located in heart of Kuvempunagar with a clear-cut patient centered vision, mission and values.
Located by Sarvodaya Main Road of Kuvempunagar, it is easily accessible. It provides medical consultation for various health issues by experienced, passionate specialists in the field and has a diagnostic medical laboratory, pharmacy and day care facilities for treatment and minor medical procedures.

Withering trust between doctor and patient today for whatever reasons, should worry us all. Recognizing them promptly and addressing them meaningfully is the need of the hour. In an attempt to bring back the lost glory, I believe the concept of good old ‘Family Doctor’ should come back. I look forward to promote and have such cordial relationship with as many families as possible who live in and around this place and strive to earn their trust to become their first choice for all their healthcare related issues and guidance for healthy happy life.

Regards,
Dr. Chandan Kumar H N
Founder of Leela Clinic
